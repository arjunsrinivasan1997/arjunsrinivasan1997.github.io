Software Engineering Intern

I worked as a Software Engineering intern in a division focused on the manufacturing of cables and circuit boards for a wide variety of customers, including the US Army, the US Navy, and Nasa

Cable Cost Neural Network

I developed a multi-layered neural network (@ 80% accuracy) that analyzed cable drawings and parts lists to estimate manufacturing cost of cables. This allowed our facility to provide estimated completion dates much more accurately for all of the products being made.

Manufacturing Process Generator

I developed an algorithm that took in raw data about a cable from various sources, and created a diagram for that cable along with a Human Readable process for the assembly of that cable. This led to new cables being able to be manufactured much more rapidly, and reduced the overall effort needed to add a new cable to the manufacturing floor.
